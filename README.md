# Dairy Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

This repository contains a Power BI report (`dairy.pbix`) created as part of my **Data Analyst portfolio**.  
The project focuses on analyzing dairy business sales performance, product demand, and regional trends to support data-driven decision-making.

## Project Overview

The dashboard analyzes dairy sales data to help stakeholders:
- Monitor overall sales and revenue performance
- Identify top- and low-performing dairy products
- Understand regional demand patterns
- Discover key factors influencing sales trends

The report is designed for business owners, sales managers, and analysts in the dairy or FMCG domain.

## Sales Performance Analysis

### Overall Sales and Revenue
- KPI cards showing total sales and revenue
- Trend analysis to evaluate business performance over time
- High-level snapshot of overall business health

### Product Performance Analysis
- Comparison of sales by dairy product types (e.g., milk, curd, butter, cheese, yogurt)
- Bar and column charts to identify top-selling and low-performing products
- Helps understand customer demand by product category

### Category Contribution Analysis
- Treemap or pie chart showing contribution of each dairy product category
- Identifies products driving the majority of revenue

### Regional and Market Analysis
- Geographic or regional visuals showing sales distribution
- Comparison of sales performance across regions or markets
- Highlights high-demand and underperforming locations

### Sales Drivers and Root-Cause Analysis
- Decomposition Tree visual to break down sales or revenue
- Analysis by product, region, or time
- Helps explain why sales increase or decline

### Influencing Factors and Advanced Insights
- Key Influencers visual to identify factors impacting sales performance
- Supports data-driven decision-making

### Interactivity and Exploration
- Slicers to filter data by product, region, or time
- Cross-filtering across visuals
- Tooltips to provide additional context on hover

## Dashboard Features

- Multiple report pages covering overview, product analysis, and advanced insights
